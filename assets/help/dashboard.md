# Home & Dashboard

The Dashboard is your home base—a quick snapshot of your reading life every time you open the app. You can choose it as your landing page under **Settings → Landing Page**.

Every section below can be turned on or off individually under **Settings → Dashboard**, so you can keep only what's useful to you.

## Continue Reading
If you read on more than one synced device, a **Continue reading** card appears when another device has read past this one—tap **Resume** to jump straight to where you left off there. The card only shows after a sync brings in a newer position, and disappears once you've caught up. Turn on **Settings → Sync → Auto sync** and the card stays fresh on its own, without pressing Sync.

## Quick Stats
A compact top row showing your **active reading streak** and other at-a-glance numbers to help you stay consistent.

## Verse of the Day
A fresh passage each day for reflection. Tap it to open the verse in the Reader.

## Reading Progress
See how much of the Bible you've read. Tap in for a detailed breakdown by book and testament.

## Reading Plans
The plans you're working through, with progress bars. Tap a plan to jump to today's reading, or open the full Reading Plans tool to manage them.

## Action Items
Your open follow-up tasks (the same ones from the Journals tab) surfaced here so nothing slips through the cracks. Check one off right from the Dashboard.

## Time Analytics
A view of how much time you've spent reading and studying over time—useful for setting and keeping study habits.

## Achievements
Milestones you've unlocked as you read, study, and journal. Tap the section to see the full list, including ones you haven't earned yet.
