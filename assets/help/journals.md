# Journals & Prayers

The Journals tab lets you track your daily walk with God, write your personal thoughts, manage your prayer list, and keep a list of follow-up action items.

## Journals
- **Daily Entries:** Tap a date on the calendar to write a journal entry for that day.
- **Distraction-free Writing:** A clean, full-height editor for capturing your thoughts. Entries save automatically as you write.
- **Revision History:** Every entry keeps past versions—tap the history icon to look back at or restore an earlier draft.
- **Tagging:** Use tags to categorize your journals (e.g. `#gratitude`, `#struggles`). You can search for tags using the global search.

> 💡 **Tip:** To quote Scripture in a journal, copy a verse from the Reader (select it and tap **Copy**) and paste it into your entry. See **Notes, Highlights & Sharing**.

> 🔗 **Auto-linking:** Type a Bible reference with its verse—like `John 3:16` or `Rom 8:28-30`—and it turns into a link automatically. Tap the link to jump straight to that passage in the Reader. Capitalize the book name (`John`, not `john`) so it's recognized.

## Prayer Tracker
- Add prayer requests to keep track of what you are praying for.
- Keep notes on how a prayer request evolves over time.
- Mark prayers as "Answered" to celebrate God's faithfulness, moving them to the Answered Prayers list!

## Action Items
The **Actions** tab is a simple to-do list for the follow-ups that come out of your study, journaling, and prayer.

- **Add an action:** Tap **+** to create one with a title and an optional description.
- **Due date & reminder:** Give an action a due date and time, and the app will surface a reminder starting a day ahead—and keep flagging it while it's overdue—until you complete or dismiss it.
- **Check them off:** Tap the checkbox to mark an action complete; completed items get a line through them. Use the **Hide Completed** switch to focus on what's still open.
- **Edit or delete:** Expand an action to reveal its description, then use **Edit** or **Delete**.
- **At a glance:** Your open actions also appear in the **Action Items** card on the Home dashboard—tap it to jump straight back here.
